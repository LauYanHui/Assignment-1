About Us

A five-piece rock band from Osaka that has a reputation for vocalist Yudai Takenaka's overwhelming singing voice that shakes the heart, solid melody work that you won't forget once you hear it, and finely detailed song arrangements. Formed in 2013 with original members. The current structure was established in January 2019 and the "Dochikusho Street Live Tour" held in July of the same year spread widely through SNS and word of mouth, quickly spreading its name across the country. Even after graduating from street live performances, they continued to make long-term hits on various streaming charts, and continued to make great strides, becoming the first indie artist to be certified as streaming by the Recording Industry Association of Japan (Gold). His major debut from Universal Music/Universal Sigma with Digital Song “Sunny drop” on August 17, 2020 at 21:00! Major 2nd Full Album “Assort” will be released on May 18, 2022! Three songs, “Walking with you,” “Tsukimisou,” and “Ai to Koi Toka” have exceeded 100 million total streaming views!
